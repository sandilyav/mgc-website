History & Legacy

Established in 1884, the Madras Gymkhana Club is a heritage institution shaped by sport, fellowship, and tradition—steadfast in spirit while evolving with each generation.

Membership Enquiries Back to Home

A Club of Continuity

Taking root as an institution for the recreation of the early British population, the Club soon admitted members of Indian birth—descended royalty and leaders of business and commerce. Codes and regulations remained unchanged over nearly a century, and were later adapted to meet the needs of a new era. Today, the Club is an eclectic mix of old and young, tradition and modern—enjoying the best of both worlds.
