A Heritage Table

Specialty dishes—like rumkin toast and pineapple passion ice cream—are part of the Club’s culinary lore and are difficult to find anywhere else. The baked dishes, cakes, and pastries are unmatchable. Even with today’s trend of outsourcing, the Club has wisely decided to only source out Chinese and North Indian cuisines, while retaining the preparation of traditional dishes within its own kitchen.
